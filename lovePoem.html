A Love Poem for You

In the symphony of life, you are my sweetest note,
With every beat of my heart, your love it wrote.
Your laughter, a melody, in my soul it floats,
A love so pure, in every word it quotes.

In the canvas of time, our story is drawn,
Each chapter with you, a new dawn.
Your eyes, the stars, in my night sky,
Guiding me with love as time goes by.

Together we dance in the moonlight's glow,
In your arms, my love continues to grow.
A love poem written in the language of our hearts,
Forever and always, we'll never be apart.

So here's my love, penned in this rhyme,
A promise to cherish you, throughout all time.
You are my muse, my heart's delight,
My love for you, an eternal light.

Forever yours,
